    _ SIZE OF THE UTERUS.

      * FIGURE OF THE UTERUS.

      * SITUATION OF THE UTERUS.

      * OF THE LIGAMENTS, TUBES, AND OVARIA, OF THE PREGNANT UTERUS.

      * OF THE BLOOD VESSELS OF THE UTERUS.

      * OF THE NERVES.
